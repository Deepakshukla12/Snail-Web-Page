The Giant African land snail (Lissachatina fulica) is one of the largest land snails, growing up to 20 centimeters. Known as a major pest, it eats many types of plants. Originally from Africa, it has spread globally and can live for years, laying hundreds of eggs annually.

A snail is a shelled gastropod. The name is most often applied to land snails,terrestrial pulmonate gastropod molluscs.

Caucasotachea vindobonensis is a large species of air-breathing land snail, a terrestrial pulmonate gastropod in the family Helicidae. The scientific name is derived from the Celtic settlement Vindobona, now known as Vienna, the capital of Austria.

A hibernating snail is not necessarily dead.

A hibernating snail may appear inactive, with its metabolic processes slowed down, but it's not necessarily dead. Hibernation is a survival strategy many animals, including snails, use to conserve energy during harsh conditions like cold winters. When conditions improve, they can awaken from hibernation.
